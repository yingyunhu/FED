About Gorillas

Two distinct passions came together in spring 2020 to form the foundation of Gorillas. One is a deep admiration of service culture, the other a love for cycling. Kağan Sümer, Founder & CEO, envisioned a world where grocery shopping would be radically faster and far more convenient, and so Gorillas was born.
